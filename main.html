Our Story

Awani is more than just a restaurant; it's a journey into the heart of Middle Eastern culture and culinary tradition. Inspired by the rich history of the region and the vibrant, bustling souks of old, our chefs craft each dish with passion and reverence. From our secret family recipes passed down through generations to the warm, inviting atmosphere, we invite you to experience a piece of our heritage. We use only the finest, locally sourced ingredients to bring you a taste of authentic, sun-kissed flavors.

Our Signature Dishes

Mandi Lamb

Slow-cooked lamb, tender and full of flavor, served over aromatic basmati rice.

Classic Hummus

Creamy, silky hummus with a hint of lemon and fresh herbs, served with warm pita bread.

Kunafeh

A traditional sweet pastry made with thin noodle-like dough, soaked in syrup and filled with cheese.
